Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecakes are a rich and decadent dessert that can be enjoyed on their own or with a variety of toppings. They come in many variations, from classic New York-style to fruity and chocolatey options. Cheesecakes are a great dessert option for special occasions, as they are impressive and delicious.

When making a cheesecake, the filling is typically made from cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, which is then baked on top of a buttery graham cracker crust. Cheesecakes can be served chilled or at room temperature and can be topped with fresh fruit, chocolate, or caramel sauce to add an extra layer of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter, then press the mixture onto the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Beat the cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture over the crust.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until the center is almost set.
  6. Let cool and then chill in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving.
