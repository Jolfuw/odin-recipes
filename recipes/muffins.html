Muffins

Description

Muffins are a popular baked good that can be enjoyed as a quick breakfast or snack. They come in a variety of flavors and can be sweet or savory, with popular options: including blueberry, banana nut, and chocolate chip. Muffins are also great for those who want a portable snack, as they can be easily wrapped and taken on the go.

When it comes to making muffins, the possibilities are endless. Different types of flour, fruits, nuts, and chocolate chips can be added to create unique and delicious muffin flavors. Muffins are typically mixed together and baked in a muffin tin until golden brown and fluffy.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil or Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: Berries, Nuts, Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, milk, oil or butter, and vanilla extract together.
  4. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Fold in any optional ingredients.
  6. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full with batter.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
